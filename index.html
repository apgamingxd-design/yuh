🛡️ Our Core Commitment: 1-Day Full Refund Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your course purchase for any reason, you may request a full 100% refund within 24 hours of your purchase date and time. No questions asked. No partial credits. A complete refund to your original payment method.

1. Overview and Scope

This Refund Policy ("Policy") governs all purchases of digital courses and educational products made through the NeuralWeave website ("Site"), operated by NeuralWeave, Inc. ("NeuralWeave," "we," "us," or "our"). By completing a purchase, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Policy in its entirety.

This Policy applies to all course products listed on the NeuralWeave Academy section of our Site. It does not apply to our AI integration services or enterprise contracts, which are governed by separate service agreements.

2. Eligibility for a Full Refund

You are eligible for a complete refund of the purchase price if all of the following conditions are met:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 24 hours of the original purchase timestamp recorded in our system.
  • The request is submitted by the original purchaser using the same email address associated with the order.
  • The purchase was made directly through the NeuralWeave Site (not through a third-party reseller or affiliate platform).
  • The course has not been fully completed (i.e., all modules and lessons marked as finished).
  • The account has not previously received a refund for the same course title.

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request within your 24-hour window, please follow these steps:

  1. Email our support team at refunds@neuralweave.ai with the subject line: "Refund Request — [Your Order ID]".
  2. Include the following in your email: your full name, the email address used at checkout, your Order ID (found in your purchase confirmation email), the course name, and the date/time of purchase.
  3. You do not need to provide a reason, but feedback is always welcome and helps us improve.
  4. Await confirmation. We will acknowledge your request within 2 business hours during normal operating hours (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST).

5. Non-Refundable Situations

The following circumstances are explicitly excluded from refund eligibility:

  • Refund requests submitted more than 24 hours after the original purchase timestamp.
  • Courses that have been fully completed (100% of all modules and lessons marked as finished).
  • Courses purchased as part of a third-party promotion, gift card redemption, or discount code where the terms stated "non-refundable."
  • Accounts found to be in violation of NeuralWeave's Terms of Service, including fraudulent chargebacks on prior orders.
  • Refund requests for the same course that has already been refunded once to the same customer.
  • Purchases made through authorized third-party resellers (contact the reseller directly for their own policies).

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

We encourage you to contact our support team directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card provider. We are committed to resolving all eligible refund requests promptly and fairly.

If a chargeback is filed for a transaction that does not meet our refund eligibility criteria, NeuralWeave reserves the right to provide documentation to your financial institution disputing the chargeback, suspend access to your account and purchased content, and pursue recovery of the disputed amount through lawful means.

7. Course Access Following a Refund

Upon approval and processing of a refund, your access to the relevant course content — including all videos, downloads, supplementary materials, and community access — will be revoked immediately. Any certificates of completion issued prior to the refund request will also be voided.

8. Technical Issues and Course Access Problems

If you are experiencing technical issues that are preventing you from accessing your course content and you have submitted a support request to support@neuralweave.ai, we will pause your 24-hour refund window for the duration of time it takes our technical team to resolve the issue, up to a maximum of 48 hours. This ensures you are not penalized for issues on our end.

9. Bundle and Multi-Course Purchases

If you purchased a bundle of courses and wish to dispute a single course within that bundle, refunds are handled on a pro-rated basis at our discretion. Please contact refunds@neuralweave.ai within the 24-hour window with your Order ID and the specific course(s) you are requesting a refund for, and our team will review your case individually.

10. Changes to This Policy

NeuralWeave reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Site and purchase of courses following any modification constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy. For purchases made prior to a policy change, the version of the Policy in effect at the time of purchase will govern your refund eligibility.
